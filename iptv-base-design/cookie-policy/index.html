Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

A cookie is a tiny text file that is automatically placed on the hard drive of your PC or mobile device by our website. These cookies allow us to identify your computer and enhance your shopping experience on our website. These cookies do not harm your computer or allow us to access your computer and do not store any personal details relating to you or your credit cards. To learn more about cookies please visit www.aboutcookies.org. This is an external website and we are not responsible for the content on it.

Why are they important?

Cookies are used to record your activity on our website. Cookies provide us with useful information on how to make our website more accessible and user friendly to you. Some of the cookies we use are essential for parts of the website to operate and without them it may alter the functionality of this website. You will still be able to browse but you won’t be able to shop on our site if you don’t enable cookies.

How to manage cookies

To get the best possible experience from our website, you will need to have cookies enabled on your computer or mobile device. Most internet browsers are automatically set up to accept cookies, but you can set your browser to refuse a cookie or ask your browser to show you where a cookie has been set up. Certain services are only activated by the presence of a cookie and, if you choose to refuse cookies, particular features of this website may not be available to you.

Find out how to disable/enable cookies by clicking on the “Manage Cookies” section of the Interactive Advertising Bureau UK website on the following link www.allaboutcookies.org

We may use Adobe Flash Player to display video or image content. It may not be possible to block or restrict flash cookies using your browser settings. However information on how to control flash player privacy and security settings is available from the Adobe website

Third Party Cookies

We advertise on other websites and our third party providers may use software than can set a cookie to your browser. These cookies allow us to monitor the effectiveness of advertising and allow us to personalise some adverts. These cookies do not harm your computer or allow us to access your computer and they do not store any personal details relating to you or your credit cards.

Personalised Adverts on other websites

We display personalised adverts on other websites within our selected network. These adverts are based on what you have been viewing on our website and may present you with alternative products that are relevant to you. These adverts rely on the use of cookies. The data contained within these cookies does not contain any personal information and we can also assure you that no personal information is passed on to the third party providers.
